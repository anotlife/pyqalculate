=== 2. UNIT CONVERSIONS ===

2.1 Speed: 3.5 miles in 12 minutes to km/h
(3.5 * miles) / (12 * minutes) to km/h
=> 28.16352 km/h


2.2 Electromagnetic: 50 Ohm * 2.5 A = Voltage
50 Ohm * 2.5 A
=> 125 V


2.3 Temperature: 98.6 F to C (manual)
(98.6 - 32) * 5/9
=> 37


2.4 Data rate: 1 Gbit/s * 1 hour to GB
1 Gbit/s * 3600 s to GB
=> 450 GB


2.5 Pressure: 14.7 psi to Pa
14.7 psi to Pa
=> 101352.9322 Pa


2.6 Mixed unit: 1.74 m to ft+in
1.74 to ft
=> 5 ft + 8.503937008 in


2.7 Energy: 1000 cal to J
1000 cal to J
=> 4184 J


2.8 Force: 100 lbf to N
100 lbf to N
=> 444.8221615 N


2.9 Volume: 1 US gallon to liters
1 gallon to L
=> 3.785411784 L


2.10 Mass: 1 stone to kg
1 stone to kg
=> 6.35029318 kg
